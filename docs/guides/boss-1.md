# Boss Name (The First Boss)

!!! danger "Mechanic: The Big Exploding Circle"
    When you get the **Red Debuff**, move to the back of the room immediately. Failure to move will result in a raid wipe.

### 🛡️ Tanks
* Swap at 3 stacks of *Heavy Smash*.
* Face the boss away from the melee group.

### ⚔️ DPS
* Focus the adds as soon as they spawn.
* Save Bloodlust/Heroism for the 30% execute phase.

### 📨 Healers
* Heavy raid-wide damage occurs during the *Scream* phase.
* Coordinate cooldowns for the second transition.
