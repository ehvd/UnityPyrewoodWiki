# 🌋 Molten Core (MC) Raid Guide

Welcome to the heart of Blackrock Mountain. This 40-man raid is the first major step into endgame content. Preparedness is 90% of the battle here—bring your **Fire Resistance (FR)** gear and plenty of consumables.

---

## 📋 Pre-Raid Checklist
- [ ] **Attunement:** Complete the "Attunement to the Core" quest (jump through the window in BRD).
- [ ] **Consumables:** Greater Fire Protection Potions (GFPP), Mongoose/Power Elixirs, and Mana Potions.
- [ ] **Fire Resistance:** Tanks should aim for $300+$ FR; Melee/Healers should have $100+$.
- [ ] **Douses:** Ensure at least 8 players have **Quintessence** (from Hydraxian Waterlords) to douse the runes.

## ⚔️ Boss Encounters

=== "Lucifron"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86829-lucifron.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The "Curse Boss." The fight is won or lost by your Decursers.

    * **The Adds:** Kill the two Flamewaker Protectors first.
    * **Mechanic:** *Lucifron's Curse* increases mana costs by 100%. **Mages/Druids must priority decurse.**
    * **Impending Doom:** Deals 2,000 Shadow damage after 10 seconds. Priests/Paladins must dispel Magic.

=== "Magmadar"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86832-magmadar.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The giant core hound.

    * **Fear:** Every 30 seconds, he uses *Panic*. Warriors must **Stance Dance** to Berserker Rage; Shamans/Paladins use Tremor Totem/Fear Ward.
    * **Tranquilizing Shot:** Hunters **must** rotate Tranq Shot to soothe his *Frenzy*.
    * **Fire Spit:** Don't stand in the lava patches on the floor.

=== "Gehennas"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/143-gehennas.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    Similar to Lucifron but with a healing debuff.

    * **Gehennas' Curse:** Reduces healing received by 75%. **Decurse this instantly.**
    * **Rain of Fire:** Move out immediately. It hits harder than the standard warlock spell.

=== "Garr"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86821-garr.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The "Add Management" fight.

    * **Firesworn Adds:** Warlocks must **Banish** as many as possible (usually 4-5).
    * **Separation:** Tanks pull the un-banished adds away from Garr. When an add dies, it knocks everyone back.
    * **Magma Shackles:** Massive slow. Dispel magic from your casters.

=== "Baron Geddon"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86835-baron-geddon.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }
    
    The "Living Bomb" and the source of the *Binding of the Windseeker*.
    
    * **Living Bomb:** If you get this, **RUN** to the designated empty area. You will explode and fly into the air. (Priests: Shield the bomb target so they don't die from fall damage).
    * **Ignite Mana:** Drains mana over time. Priests/Paladins must dispel.
    * **Inferno:** Geddon stays still and pulses massive AoE fire damage. **Melee: Run out!**

=== "Shazzrah"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86833-shazzrah.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The "Arcane Blink" boss.

    * **Blink:** He frequently blinks into the middle of the raid. Tanks must quickly taunt him back.
    * **Arcane Explosion:** Huge AoE damage after he blinks. Ranged/Healers must move away immediately.
    * **Magic Dampening:** He buffs himself to reduce magic damage taken; Mages must use **Spellsteal** or Priests must **Dispel**.


=== "Sulfuron"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86834-sulfuron-harbinger.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The "Healer" fight.

    * **The Adds:** He has 4 Flamewaker Priests. They **must** be pulled away from the boss and interrupted/stunned.
    * **Healing:** The adds will try to heal each other and the boss. Assign an interrupt rotation to stop every cast.
    * **Shadow Word: Pain:** Priests/Paladins must dispel the heavy DoT on the raid.

=== "Golemagg"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/86827-golemagg-the-incinerator-old-model.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    A heavy tank-and-spank with a focus on pet management.

    * **Core Ragers:** He has two dogs. They cannot be killed (they revive) until Golemagg dies.
    * **The Strategy:** Two Off-Tanks pull the dogs away from the boss. They must be kept separated to avoid a buff.
    * **Melt Armor:** Reduces the main tank's armor. Healers must be ready for spike damage as the stacks increase.

=== "Majordomo"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/147-majordomo-executus.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The only boss you don't actually kill. You win by killing all his adds.

    * **The Adds:** 4 Flamewaker Healers and 4 Flamewaker Elites.
    * **Strategy:** Use **Polymorph** on the Healers (Mages are key here). Focus down the Elites first, then the Healers.
    * **Reflection Shields:** Majordomo alternates between a **Magic Reflection** shield and a **Damage Reflection** shield. Watch the buff icons or you will kill yourself!
    * **The Cache:** Once all adds die, Majordomo surrenders. You loot the "Cache of the Firelord" (where the Leaf/Sinew for Hunters drops).


=== "Ragnaros"

    ![Image title](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/screenshots/normal/1061-ragnaros-ragnaros-after-killing-us.jpg){ loading=lazy, width=256, align=left }

    The Firelord himself. You have **10 minutes** to kill him before he submerges.

    | Phase | Mechanic | Strategy |
    | :--- | :--- | :--- |
    | **P1: The Fight** | *Wrath of Ragnaros* | Knocks back melee. High FR prevents the "knock-up" damage. |
    | | *Hand of Ragnaros* | Knocks back everyone near him. Stay spread out to avoid chain-knocks. |
    | | *Magma Splash* | Triggered if no one is in melee range. A tank **must** always be hitting him. |
    | **P2: Submerge** | *Sons of Flame* | 8 adds spawn. Warlocks Banish, Mages AoE Frost Nova. Kill them fast! |

    !!! warning

        **Ranged & Healers:** Do not stand near the lava edges. If you get knocked into the lava, you lose precious DPS/Healing time and will likely die to fire ticks.
