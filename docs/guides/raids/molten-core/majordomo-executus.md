The only boss you don't actually kill. You win by killing all his adds.

* **The Adds:** 4 Flamewaker Healers and 4 Flamewaker Elites.
* **Strategy:** Use **Polymorph** on the Healers (Mages are key here). Focus down the Elites first, then the Healers.
* **Reflection Shields:** Majordomo alternates between a **Magic Reflection** shield and a **Damage Reflection** shield. Watch the buff icons or you will kill yourself!
* **The Cache:** Once all adds die, Majordomo surrenders. You loot the "Cache of the Firelord" (where the Leaf/Sinew for Hunters drops).
