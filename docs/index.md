## About Us

We’re a community-driven guild founded by players who value collaboration over control. We’re not here to raid 7 nights
a week or chase parses at the cost of anything. We're here to build a lasting community, where every member feels
welcome, heard, and be part of something bigger than themselves. 

### Efficient Progression, Zero Sweat 
We raid smart, not hard. Our goal is to clear all content efficiently without leaning on world buffs, stacks of
consumables, or trivializing fights with overgear. We believe in mastering mechanics and doing things the right way —
not just throwing gear, buffs at the problem. 

### Mentorship & Growth 
We take pride in teaching fights, helping players improve their class knowledge, and investing in people who want to get
better. Our raid leaders and mentors are here to elevate.
Community & Events 
Raiding is just one part of the experience. We’ll run fun events, community game nights, and even work on building
cross-guild collaborations across the server. This isn’t just about our raid group — it’s about growing a larger, more
connected server community. We all just want to enjoy playing the same lovely game. 

### Ego-Free Leadership 
No power trips here. Our officers don’t see themselves as bosses, but as facilitators. We value feedback, and everyone
gets a voice. Bring your friends, share your ideas, and help shape the future of the guild. 

### Growing Together 
We’re still building. As we continue to expand, we’ll be pugging some raid spots — but the goal is to establish a
strong, reliable core group that shares our values and vision. If you’re someone who wants to help lay the foundation
for something meaningful, now’s the perfect time to get involved. 

Let’s create something that lasts — together.
