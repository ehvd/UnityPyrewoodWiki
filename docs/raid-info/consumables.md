## :material-test-tube: Consumables & World Buffs (WB)

Expectations vary based on the raid's difficulty:

| Raid             | World Buffs      | High-End Consumes      | Low-End Consumes |
|:-----------------|:-----------------|:-----------------------|:-----------------|
| **Naxxramas**    | No (Progression) | Mandatory (Boss Kills) | Mandatory        |
| **AQ40 / BWL**   | Yes (Unboon)     | Expected               | Mandatory        |
| **MC / 20-Mans** | Optional         | Optional               | Mandatory        |

### World Buff Priority:

* **Mandatory:** Onyxia/Nefarian & Hakkar.
* **Optional:** DMT, Rend, Songflower.
