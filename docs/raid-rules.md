# Raid Rules & Expectations

### 📅 Schedule
* **Tuesday:** 8:00 PM – 11:00 PM EST
* **Thursday:** 8:00 PM – 11:00 PM EST
* *Optional Heroic/Alt Run: Sunday 8:00 PM EST*

### 🎒 Preparation
* **Consumables:** Phials, Potions, and Food Buffs are **required** for every pull.
* **Addons:** You must have **BigWigs/DBM**, **WeakAuras**, and **Method Raid Tools** installed.
* **Research:** Review the [Boss Guides](guides/boss-1.md) before the first night of progression.

### 💰 Loot System
We use **Loot Council** via the *RCLootCouncil* addon. Distribution is based on:
1. Size of Upgrade
2. Attendance & Reliability
3. Performance & Attitude
